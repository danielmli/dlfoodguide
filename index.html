Part I: Table Service and Mobile Order

Go directly to the food

Three types of places you can get food at:

  • stalls (e.g. popcorn, churros, pretzels, turkey legs)
  • quick-service (order at counter, get food, find somewhere to sit)
  • table-service (host stand, server, whole deal)

Realistically speaking you'll be eating at quick-service restaurants because of several reasons:

  1. relatively good 性价比. table-service food is generally pretty overpriced
  2. it's conveient and located all around
  3. it's impossible to get reservations for the good table-service restaurants

reservations are made by people up to 60 days in advance through the app (speaking of which, GET THE APP). You can also join walk-up lists for the table-service restaurants through the app, but those are really really hard to get. If you are trying though, here are generally the top tier ones to try for:

  • Lamplight Lounge (DCA)
  • Café Orleans (DL)
  • Blue Bayou (DL)
  • Oga's Cantina (DL)
  • Carthay Circle (DCA)

Mobile Ordering

REMINDER AGAIN TO DOWNLOAD THE APP AND REGISTER!!!! Majority of quick-service places also do mobile ordering. You pick a time frame, order through your phone, and avoid the lines basically. pretty nice.

Pt II: Disneyland

Here are the places in Disneyland, what I like from them, and stuff. If it's not here assume I haven't eaten it

Bengal Barbeque

Solid. Pork Belly and Bacon-wrapped asparagus are favs. don't bother with chicken

Tiki Juice Bar

Famous for pineapple dole whip. Good for your lactose friends. Pretty good by itself but i'm not a fan of the float

Harbour Galley

Bread bowls are a good bet (it's mostly bread but it's good). Heard the Lobster Rolls were pretty good but haven't tried

Edelweiss Snacks

Turkey Legs are a classic but you can get them elsewhere. Chimichangas are surprisingly good

Stage Door Café

Bruh Disneyland has the best corn dogs. get them here or at the Red Wagon near main st usa.

Plaza Inn

Fried chicken and grilled cheese w/ tomato basil soup is good

French Market

My old go-to. Bread Bowls are pretty good and filling

Mint Julep Bar

Beignets are good and Instagrammable. Mint Juleps are a really sweet minty lemonade. I like it.

Docking Bay 7

Beef Pot roast is pretty good. Only thing I've tried though.

Milk Stand

lol these are fucking weird but if you're a star wars fan u should try them for funsies.

Pizza Planet

Don't bother. cafeteria-level pizza

Galaxtic Grill

Solidly Okay. Burgers are solid but you're paying $12–$13 for something the size of a McDonalds Big Mac

Churro Carts

Disneyland sells the best churros and you can't convince me otherwise. No need to bother with the special ones some carts sell. the OG is good enuf

Pt III: California Adventure (DCA)

for alc. The regulator is good (mango w/ a bit of spice). Molecular Meldown is good if u like stouts. DO NOT GET PARTICLE FIZZ

Pym Test Kitchen

Pretty solid. Pym-ini Sandwich and chicken sandwhich is good. Impossible Spoonful is very very average. Don't bother with Quantum pretzel unless instagramming because it's fucking huge - if u really want a pretzel get an atomic fusion pretzel instead or a plain one from a cart.

Schwarma Palace

Both options here are REALLY GOOD but REALLY EXPENSIVE for what they are. If the one inside avengers campus has too long a line there is another cart in front of Hyperion theatre.
